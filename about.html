Who We Are

The Institution was established under the objective to provide wholistic courses in the guidance of Biblical guidance. Dr. Karanja together with a team of dedicated professionals, founded St. Michaels Vocational Training Institute in 2025. Over the years, we have expanded our programs to include a wide range of vocational skills, that empower individuals to excel in their careers while upholding Christian values.

Our Mission

Our mission is to uphold and advance biblical, ethical, and professional standards in Christian leadership and vocational training. We aim to equip the Christian Faith ministers-in-training morally, physically, mentally, and spiritually, producing leaders who serve with integrity, spiritual empowerment, and excellence. Through collaboration, advocacy, research, and resource mobilization, APVOTIK strives to raise the quality of Christian ministry and vocational training — locally and internationally.

Statement of Faith

We are rooted in Pentecostal theology and guided by:

  • The authority of The Scripture.
  • Salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ.
  • The baptism and empowerement of the Holy Spirit.
  • The importance of the local church in fulfilling the Great Commission.
  • The call to serve the marginalized and promote social justice.
  • The importance of character and integrity in leadership.
  • The call to evangelism and discipleship

Our Vision

To be a leading vocational institute recognized for excellence in skills training, character development, and community transformation through Christ-centered education.
